About Vision Music

Vision Entertainment is a Addis Ababa–based media company dedicated to Oromo music — from seasoned legends to the new generation. Vision Music is our Android app: a modern, purpose-built home for that catalog.

One app. One sound. Songs you grew up with and songs that will soundtrack where we go next — presented in a player designed for how people actually listen today.
