Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-23
Contact: support@visionmusic.app
App: Vision Music (Android package com.visionmusic.app)

This policy describes how Vision Music ("we", "our", "the app"), an Android application published by Vision Entertainment, handles information when you use the app. Vision Music is distributed through the Google Play Store.

1. Information we may collect

Vision Music is designed to collect the minimum information needed to operate. Depending on the features you use, the app may collect:

  • Account information — if you sign in with Google, we receive your email address, display name, and a unique Google account identifier, used to associate your library and preferences with your account.
  • App activity — basic interactions with the app (songs played, items saved to your library, searches performed) used to provide playback and continuity across sessions.
  • App info and performance — crash reports and diagnostic information used to detect and fix bugs.
  • Device identifiers — a non-resettable or resettable identifier used for analytics, abuse prevention, and session stability.
  • Approximate region — derived from your network connection, used to apply content availability rules where applicable.

We do not access your contacts, SMS messages, call logs, photos, precise location, microphone, or camera.

2. Information we do not collect

We do not sell personal data. We do not use personal data for advertising targeting. The app does not currently display third-party advertising.

3. How we use information

  • Provide music streaming and playback features.
  • Keep your library, listening history, and preferences available across sessions.
  • Detect crashes, diagnose bugs, and improve reliability.
  • Comply with legal obligations and content licensing requirements.

4. Sharing

We share information only with service providers that help us operate the app — in particular Google Firebase (authentication, Cloud Firestore, and Cloud Storage) — which process data on our behalf under Google's terms. We may disclose information to authorities when legally required (for example, in response to a valid court order).

We do not share personal data with third parties for their own marketing.

5. Data retention & deletion

  • Account data is retained while your account is active.
  • Usage and diagnostic logs are retained for up to 24 months and then deleted or anonymized.
  • You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time. See our account deletion page for instructions, or email support@visionmusic.app. We will complete deletion within 30 days unless we are legally required to retain specific records.

6. Children

Vision Music is intended for users aged 18 and over. The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, contact support@visionmusic.app and we will delete it.

7. Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under laws such as the EU/UK GDPR or the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), including the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Export your data in a portable format.
  • Object to, or restrict, certain processing.

To exercise any of these rights, contact support@visionmusic.app. We will respond within the timeframes required by applicable law.

8. Security

We use industry-standard measures to protect your data, including encryption in transit (HTTPS) and restricted administrative access. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

9. International transfers

Because Vision Music relies on Google Firebase services, your information may be processed and stored in data centers outside your country of residence. Google provides appropriate safeguards for these transfers under its standard terms.

10. Permissions

The Android app requests the following permissions:

  • Internet / Network state — to stream music and sync with the service.
  • Foreground service (media playback) — to keep music playing when the app is in the background.
  • Post notifications — to show playback controls in the notification shade.
  • Wake lock — to keep audio playing without the screen being on.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated version at this URL and update the effective date at the top. Material changes will be highlighted in the app or by email where appropriate.

12. Contact

Questions, requests, or complaints:
Email: support@visionmusic.app

If you are not satisfied with our response, you may have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.
