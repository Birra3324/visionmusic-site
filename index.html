About Vision Music

Vision Entertainment is an Addis Ababa–based media company dedicated to Oromo and Ethiopian music — from seasoned legends to the new generation. Vision Music is our streaming app: a modern, purpose-built home for that catalog.

One app. One sound. Songs you grew up with and songs that will soundtrack where we go next — presented in a player designed for how people actually listen today. With lyrics, queue management, and smart discovery tabs, Vision Music keeps getting better.
